Gateway Horizon International

This comprehensive branding project involved developing a complete identity package for Gateway Horizon International, a company specializing in international trade and packaging solutions. The client needed a full brand presence to establish credibility in the global market, including website, logo, business cards, and marketing materials.

I designed a distinctive logo that represented the company's global reach and packaging focus, created professional business cards that reinforced the brand identity, and developed a visually compelling marketing brochure to showcase their services and capabilities.

For the digital presence, I implemented a clean, corporate website design with intuitive navigation and clear calls-to-action to improve user engagement and lead conversion. The website features comprehensive service pages, an about section highlighting company expertise, and a contact system that makes it easy for potential clients to reach out. The site is fully responsive and optimized for search engines.

Sunshine Hypnosis Academy Website

This client project involved creating a complete online presence for a new hypnosis training academy. I worked closely with the client to understand their brand values, target audience, and business goals.

The design focused on creating a calming yet professional atmosphere that would inspire trust in potential students. I implemented a user-friendly course registration system and optimized the site for search engines to increase organic traffic.

Cancer Detection Project

This project represents my deep dive into medical AI applications. I trained a convolutional neural network on thousands of histopathological images to identify malignant tissue patterns with over 92% accuracy.

The challenging aspect of this project was balancing model complexity with performance. I implemented data augmentation techniques to improve training with limited medical datasets and utilized transfer learning with pre-trained models to achieve better results.

The web interface allows medical professionals to upload scans and receive immediate analysis results, potentially speeding up diagnosis processes in resource-limited settings.
